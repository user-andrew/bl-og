about

Established in June 2020, our Cardano stake pool has been around since the Cardano mainnet launch. We have now minted over 400 mainnet blocks, and have provided our delegators with over 400,000 ADA in rewards.

Our stake pool consists of 4 nodes: 1 block producer, 2 relays, and 1 back-up block producer. Each node has 4 CPUs, 8GB of RAM, 100% SSD storage, and 99.99% server uptime. Our nodes can easily be upgraded to higher specs as the protocol demands.

In our spare time, we like to create educational YouTube content highlighting positive aspects of Cardano.

when

When are rewards distributed? Rewards are paid out every 5 days at the end of an Epoch. However, if this is your first time delegating to any Cardano stake pool, then there is a slight delay from when you start delegating to when you start receiving rewards (15 - 20 days). Please see the chart below for guidance.


If, for example, you delegated in Epoch 253 (11th - 16th March 21:45 UTC), then your ADA becomes "active" during Epoch 255. And rewards for Epoch 255 will be paid out on the 31st of March 21:45 UTC. For delegators joining us from other stake pools, there is no gap in rewards as you should continue to receive rewards from your old pool until you start receiving rewards from our pool.

policy

Our fees are set to the minimum 340 ADA fixed fee, and 1% variable fee. Our variable fee will gradually increase over time as the pool becomes more saturated. This fee will always be reasonable, always be competitive, and never be over 5%.
